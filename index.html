Questions

Things CEOs ask before the first call.

How long is a typical engagement?

Retainers typically run 6 to 18 months.

How many clients do you take at a time?

Momentum keeps capacity intentionally limited — no more than three active Fully Engaged retainers at once. That's how every client gets real leadership.

What size institution is the right fit?

Banks and credit unions from $100M to $10B in assets, along with newly launched and established digital banking brands.

Do you work with agencies or vendors?

Yes. Part of fractional leadership is evaluating and managing marketing partners as an extension of your team.

How is this different from a consulting firm?

Firms sell decks and advice — often leaving you to gain internal acceptance, and find the time and capacity to implement on your own.

What if my team is resistant to outside leadership?

That's common. The role works because it's not about replacing your team — it's about amplifying what they're capable of and removing obstacles. We start with listening, understanding what they care about, and building credibility. Most teams become advocates once they see the difference clear strategy makes.
